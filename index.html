About me

I am a passionate and creative BSIT student majoring in Animation and Motion Graphics, with a strong foundation in visual storytelling and digital design. My expertise spans across various cutting-edge software, including Blender for 3D modeling and animation, Lumion for architectural visualization, and SketchUp for precise and dynamic 3D designs. I am also skilled in using Canva for graphic design and marketing materials, and Photoshop for high-quality photo editing and digital art.

With a deep interest in transforming imaginative ideas into visually compelling experiences, I continually hone my skills to stay at the forefront of the evolving digital design landscape. My technical proficiency, combined with my artistic sensibilities, drives me to create both functional and aesthetically pleasing visuals, whether for animation, motion graphics, or design projects. I look forward to leveraging these skills in real-world applications, contributing to projects that push the boundaries of digital creativity.
